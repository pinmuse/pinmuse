Connect your Pinterest account and browse your saved fashion inspiration boards directly within the app.

About PinMuse

PinMuse is a fashion technology app designed to help users turn inspiration into wearable everyday outfits using artificial intelligence and personal wardrobe matching.

PinMuse is an independent application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Pinterest.
