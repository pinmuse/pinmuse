Welcome to PinMuse. This Privacy Policy explains how PinMuse ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal information when you use the PinMuse mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service"). By using PinMuse, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

PinMuse is an independent application and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Pinterest.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal and non-personal information:

  • Account information: your email address when you register or sign in to PinMuse.
  • Pinterest connection data: when you connect your Pinterest account, we access OAuth tokens, your selected boards, and the pins you have saved to those boards. We do not store your Pinterest password.
  • Pinterest boards and pins: content from boards you explicitly select for use within PinMuse, including pin images and metadata.
  • Wardrobe images: photographs of clothing items you upload to build your digital wardrobe within the app.
  • Style preferences: preferences and settings you configure within the app (e.g., preferred styles, color palettes, occasions).
  • Approximate location: coarse location data (city/region level) used solely to provide weather-appropriate outfit recommendations. Precise GPS coordinates are not stored.
  • Usage data: anonymized information about how you interact with the app, such as features used and session duration, for product improvement.
  • Device information: device type, operating system version, and app version for debugging and support purposes.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the PinMuse Service.
  • Match your Pinterest-saved looks with items in your personal wardrobe.
  • Generate AI-powered outfit recommendations personalized to you.
  • Deliver weather-aware styling suggestions based on your approximate location.
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset).
  • Respond to support requests and inquiries.
  • Analyze aggregate usage patterns to improve the app experience.
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising profiling.

3. Pinterest Integration

PinMuse integrates with the Pinterest API to allow you to connect your Pinterest account and access your saved fashion boards and pins. When you connect your Pinterest account:

  • We request only the OAuth scopes necessary to read the boards and pins you explicitly select for use within PinMuse.
  • Your Pinterest access tokens are stored securely and used solely to retrieve content you have authorized.
  • You may disconnect your Pinterest account from PinMuse at any time through the app settings or directly via Pinterest's connected apps settings at pinterest.com/settings/security.
  • Upon disconnection, we will delete your stored Pinterest tokens. Cached pin data may be retained for a short period before automatic deletion.

Your use of Pinterest through PinMuse is also subject to Pinterest's Privacy Policy.

4. AI Services

PinMuse uses artificial intelligence and machine learning services to analyze clothing images and generate outfit recommendations. This may involve transmitting images and metadata to third-party AI providers (such as OpenAI or similar services).

  • Image data sent to AI providers is used solely for the purpose of generating outfit analysis and recommendations within your session.
  • We select AI providers that offer appropriate data processing agreements and do not use submitted data for training third-party models without consent.
  • AI-generated suggestions are recommendations only. PinMuse does not guarantee the accuracy or suitability of any styling recommendation.

5. Location Data

PinMuse requests access to your device's approximate location (city or region level) in order to retrieve current weather conditions and provide seasonally appropriate outfit suggestions.

  • Location is requested only when you use the weather-based styling feature.
  • Precise GPS coordinates are not stored on our servers.
  • You may deny location access at any time through your device settings. Denying location access will disable weather-based outfit recommendations but will not affect other app functionality.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers provided by reputable cloud hosting providers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures — including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest — to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the Service or as required by applicable law. When you delete your account, your personal data will be deleted within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Third-party services we may use include:

  • Pinterest API — for board and pin access.
  • OpenAI or similar AI providers — for image analysis and outfit generation.
  • Weather API providers — for current weather data based on location.
  • Cloud hosting providers — for secure data storage and app infrastructure.

Each of these providers is governed by their own privacy policy and data processing terms. We encourage you to review their policies.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: you may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: you may request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Deletion: you may request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten"), subject to legal retention requirements.
  • Withdrawal of consent: where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Data portability: you may request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: you may object to processing of your data for certain purposes.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law (typically 30 days).
